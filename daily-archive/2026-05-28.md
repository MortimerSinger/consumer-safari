NielsenIQ now projects $190B-$385B in U.S. agentic e-commerce spend by 2030. James Murdoch's Lupa Systems acquired New York Magazine and the Vox Media Podcast Network for $300M.

## AI

### 🛍️ AI & Commerce
- **AWS launches Agentic Shopping Assistant for retailers — Kate Spade live, more in testing** (About Amazon) — Productizes the Alexa for Shopping (Rufus) stack for non-Amazon retailers via AWS, with Kate Spade as the first live customer (built on Anthropic Haiku 4.5 through Amazon Bedrock AgentCore). AWS pitches deployment in ~60 days. Rufus drove ~$12B in incremental Amazon sales last year. [link](https://www.aboutamazon.com/news/aws/aws-agentic-shopping-assistant-retailers) `Agentic commerce`
- **Google unveils Universal Cart + Agent Payment Protocol (AP2) at I/O** (LinkedIn (Adam Silverman analysis)) — Universal Cart aggregates products across Search, Gemini, YouTube, and Gmail into a single cart that tracks deals and price drops. Built on the Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP). AP2 lets AI agents make purchases autonomously within defined spending limits. [link](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/walmarts-hot-targets-back-google-wants-your-cart-still-silverman-9tnue) `Agentic checkout`
- **NIQ: agentic shoppers could capture $190B-$385B in U.S. e-commerce by 2030** (FoodNavigator-USA (citing NielsenIQ)) — NIQ's 'Commerce Revolution: Where East Meets West' report cites Morgan Stanley's $190B-$385B U.S. agentic spend forecast (10-20% of online retail) by 2030, with McKinsey projecting $3T-$5T globally. NIQ urges brands to invest in AI-readable product data now or risk being skipped. [link](https://www.foodnavigator-usa.com/Article/2026/05/28/how-ai-is-merging-eastern-and-western-commerce-into-one-intelligent-ecosystem/) `Market sizing`
