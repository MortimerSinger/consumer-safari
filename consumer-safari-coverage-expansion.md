## Themes the Cron Should Systematically Hunt

Beyond deal headlines and earnings, these are recurring operator/investor themes worth surfacing:

### Regulatory & operational
- **EPR (Extended Producer Responsibility) packaging regulation** — 7 US states implementing, 2026 reporting deadlines, SKU-level packaging data requirements
- **FTC greenwashing crackdowns** — recyclability claims, "eco" and "clean" substantiation
- **Label compliance** — allergen, nutrition, sustainability disclosure modernization

### Consumer / category dynamics
- **Protein-forward / functional** as a share-gaining category across snacks, beverages, ready-to-drink
- **Premium convenience** — weeknight meals with elevated flavor and ingredients for time-poor, quality-focused shoppers
- **"Worth it" brands** — value trading down on baskets but paying up for functional benefits
- **Private label depth** — Circana-style metrics on dollar share, unit share, Gen Z adoption, club channel
- **"Mushy middle" avoidance** — brands must be clearly value OR clearly premium/functional

### Capital markets & deal dynamics
- **Smaller-check, higher-frequency deal thesis** — 2026 shifting from megadeals to more frequent strategic bolt-ons in beauty, F&B, lifestyle
- **Institutional readiness as gating factor** — strategics and PE rewarding operationally mature brands over growth stories
- **Deal process intelligence** — acquisitions start months/years before announcement; competitive intelligence and data sourcing
- **Restaurant refranchising patterns** — Krispy Kreme/WKS, Roark/NBC, company-to-franchisee portfolio moves
- **Hospitality real estate financing** — portfolio repositioning deals ($286M 38-hotel type transactions)
- **Earnings-week pre-reads** — what to watch in upcoming reports, not just what happened

### AI beyond commerce
- **AI for assortment / promo / innovation** — NPD decisions powered by Tastewise-type data
- **AI re-employment gap** — Goldman finding that job-finding rates are declining in AI-exposed occupations
- **Retail media + AI performance storytelling** — outperforming broad "better-for-you" narratives
